What is guaranteed media placement?

Guaranteed media placement is editorial PR with a contractual commitment: your story runs in a publication you approve before you pay, refunded if not. Baden Bower has run this model since 2018, publishing 25,000+ articles for 3,548 clients across Forbes, Vogue, Business Insider and 700+ outlets.

Most agencies sell hours. We sell outcomes. The difference is whether you have a Forbes article on launch day or a six month retainer and a folder of polite no-replies.

Questions · answered plainly

Common questions

Q1.Is this sponsored content or earned media?

The article runs as editorial coverage under the publication's normal byline conventions, not as a labelled advertisement. Where an outlet requires a disclosure, we tell you before you approve it.

Q2.How long does a placement take?

Typically 14 to 28 days from kickoff to publication, and once you approve the final article it goes live within 72 hours. Tier-1 mastheads sometimes take 4 to 6 weeks. We give you the realistic window on the strategy call, in writing.

Q3.Do you write the article?

Yes. Our editors craft the angle and draft the copy in the outlet's house voice. You review and approve before it reaches an editor's inbox.

Q4.What does it cost?

One agency program, four levels of firepower. Establish starts from $9,000 a month on the 12-month program billed annually, Authority from $13,500, and Dominant from $18,000, with quarterly and monthly billing also available. Agency of Record engagements start from $35,000 a month, scoped by proposal. Every program includes guaranteed features, the full earned-media program and the money-back guarantee. The full breakdown is on the pricing page.

Q5.Who is Baden Bower for?

Business owners seeking media credibility to increase conversions, executives building thought leadership, authors and speakers pursuing keynotes, and O-1 or EB-1A visa applicants who need published coverage as documented evidence.

Q6.Will my coverage show up in AI answers?

That is the point of the model. ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini cite authored editorial far more than wire distribution, and every placement is permanently indexed. We track where the engines cite you, and where they cite competitors instead.

Q7.What if an outlet becomes unavailable?

We substitute an outlet of equivalent standing, agreed with you first, or refund that placement. Availability shifts month to month, which is why the outlet list is mapped on the strategy call.
